Purpose

The NYU Abu Dhabi office of Student Affairs sponsors weekend transportation to UAE-based resident students. This service is managed by Facilities Management.

We aim to emphasize the importance of responsible communication and respect for time among the NYUAD community.

Definition of Terms

  • Late Cancellation: A student who informs the NYUAD transportation staff of their inability to take the provided transport within fewer than 24 hours of departure from NYUAD on Friday at 6pm.
  • No-show: A student who fails to communicate with nyuad.transport@nyu.edu within the 24-hour window of cancellation. Two “No-shows” will result in those students arranging their own transportation for the remainder of the semester.

Registration and Rules

Students must fill out the online form found on the student portal each week to avail of the university-sponsored transport. Please keep the following in mind:

  • Only registered UAE residents can utilize this service. Guests are not permitted.
  • Students must register by 10am on Thursday each week. Late requests will not be acknowledged.
  • Facilities Management reserves the right to decline service for poor conduct.
  • No alterations to the transportation schedule are allowed.
  • Students are responsible for any vehicle damage.
  • Extenuating circumstances will be considered on a case-by-case basis.

Timings for Weekend Transportation

Transportation departs Saadiyat campus, Welcome Center at 6pm on Friday. Pick-up times on Sunday vary based on location.

Students must arrive 15 minutes before departure. Late arrivals will not be accommodated.

Note: Once you are dropped off at the Corniche Al Qawasim, you can easily find a taxi or use ride-hailing services like Uber or Careem to reach your booked accommodation. The Corniche is conveniently located in the heart of the city, making transportation to your destination quick and accessible.
