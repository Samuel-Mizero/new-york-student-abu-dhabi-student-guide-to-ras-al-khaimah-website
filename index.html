Ready to take a break from Saadiyat Island’s study sessions and find new inspiration?

Or

Looking for a refreshing break from the fast-paced university routine?

Ras Al Khaimah (RAK) is calling!

Just a few hours away, this northern gem of the UAE offers the perfect escape to recharge and unwind. With its stunning landscapes and rich culture, RAK promises a unique experience. Whether you’re bonding with friends or seeking adventure, a different side of the country awaits you

But #why you for RAK?

A Study Break Worth Taking

Tired of library views? Imagine prepping for exams by a serene beach or brainstorming ideas while overlooking the Hajar Mountains.

Quick and Easy Adventure

Whether it’s a weekend road trip with friends or a solo day to explore, RAK is just the right distance from Abu Dhabi for a change of pace without straying too far from campus life.

Hiking for All Skill Levels

Conquer the UAE’s tallest mountain, Jebel Jais, or enjoy beginner-friendly hiking trails that make the journey as memorable as the destination.

And #what to do at RAK?

Outdoor Enthusiasts

Swap the Saadiyat boardwalk for mountain treks, ziplining over canyons, or paddleboarding through mangroves.

Cultural Seekers

Visit the historic Al Dhayah Fort, explore the National Museum of Ras Al Khaimah, or step into traditional Emirati culture at local souks.

Relaxation Lovers

If you need to unwind, enjoy beachfront resorts, sunset yoga, or even camping under the stars with friends from campus.

Plan your RAK #getaway today!

Whether you're a first-year or nearing graduation, RAK offers NYUAD students a perfect balance of relaxation and adventure. Gather your classmates, pack your essentials, and make memories that will last beyond your time at university.
