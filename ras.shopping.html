Manar Mall +

Unleash your shopping spirit at Manar Mall, the largest and most popular shopping destination in Ras Al Khaimah! Dive into a vibrant mix of international and local retail brands, catering to every style and budget. Enjoy a delightful dining experience with diverse options in the food court, featuring beloved chains and local favorites.

But that’s not all! Catch the latest blockbusters at the cinema, let your kids play in the fun-filled play areas, or stroll along the stunning waterfront promenade for a relaxing break. With a massive Virgin store and countless choices at your fingertips, Manar Mall is your ultimate destination for shopping, dining, and entertainment! Come and explore the endless possibilities today!

Al Hamra Mall +

Experience the charm of Al Hamra Mall, conveniently located near Al Hamra Village! This modern shopping haven caters to both residents and tourists, featuring a delightful mix of high-end and mid-range brands. Recently renovated in 2024, the mall offers a fresh and inviting atmosphere.

Indulge your taste buds with an array of dining options, including favorites like Five Guys and Pizza Express, alongside other popular food brands. Plus, enjoy the latest films at the cinema for a perfect day out. Whether you're shopping or dining, Al Hamra Mall promises an unforgettable experience for everyone!

Al Aswaq RAK National Markets +

Discover Al Aswaq RAK National Markets, a vibrant shopping destination in Ras Al Khaimah that beautifully blends traditional and modern retail experiences! This bustling hub attracts locals and visitors alike, offering a diverse range of affordable products.

Immerse yourself in the local charm as you explore stalls and small shops reminiscent of a traditional souq, delivering an authentic Emirati shopping experience. From unique finds to everyday essentials, Al Aswaq RAK National Markets promises an unforgettable adventure for all who visit!

Al Naeem Mall +

Explore Al Naeem Mall, a premier shopping destination in Ras Al Khaimah that combines modern convenience with a delightful shopping experience! Nestled near the beautiful Ras Al Khaimah Corniche, this mall offers the perfect opportunity to enjoy a leisurely stroll along the waterfront after a day of shopping.

With a wide range of retail options and a welcoming atmosphere, Al Naeem Mall is your go-to spot for both retail therapy and relaxation. Come and discover the ideal blend of shopping and scenic beauty today!
